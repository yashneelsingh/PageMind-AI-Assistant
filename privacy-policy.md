# Privacy Policy for PageMind (AI Research Assistant)

**Last Updated:** March 24, 2026

Thank you for choosing to use the PageMind Chrome Extension ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy.

This privacy notice describes how we might collect, store, use, and/or share ("process") your information when you use our extension.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Personal Information You Disclose to Us
We **do not** collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, physical address, or phone number. We do not require you to create an account to use the core features of the extension.

### B. Information Automatically Collected
When you actively use the extension to summarize or ask questions about a webpage or PDF, we collect:
*   **Webpage Content:** The text and title of the specific webpage you are currently reading, but *only* when you explicitly request a summary or ask a question.
*   **URLs:** The URL of the page you are analyzing to provide context and caching for the AI.
*   **Query Data:** The text of the questions you type into the extension.

**We do not track your browsing history in the background.** Data is only processed on the exact pages you choose to run the extension on.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We process your information for the sole purpose of providing, improving, and administering our services:
*   **AI Processing:** The text of the webpage, the URL, and your questions are securely transmitted to our backend servers to generate AI summaries and answers.
*   **Caching:** We may temporarily cache the processed text of popular URLs to provide faster summaries for future requests (without tying it to any specific user).

## 3. Will Your Information Be Shared with Anyone?

We only share information with the following third parties:
*   **Google Gemini (or AI Provider):** The webpage text and your queries are sent to our language model provider strictly to generate the response.
*   **Database Providers:** Temporary data related to specific URLs is stored securely in our MongoDB instance to support the Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) feature.

We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

## 4. How Long Do We Keep Your Information?

We keep your information only as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this privacy notice. Cached webpage data may be periodically cleared from our servers.

## 5. Security of Your Information

We have implemented appropriate technical and organizational security measures designed to protect the security of any information we process. However, despite our safeguards and efforts to secure your information, no electronic transmission over the Internet or information storage technology can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.
